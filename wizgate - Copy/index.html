Our Story

From Consultancy to Language Mastery

Wizgate Language Academy is a proud subsidiary of Wizgate International — an established leader in global education and consultancy. Founded in 2019, Wizgate International has spent five years building a reputation for excellence in bridging the gap between students and European opportunities.

While our roots are in helping students integrate through the Polish language, we are rapidly evolving into a multi-language hub for the global community.
